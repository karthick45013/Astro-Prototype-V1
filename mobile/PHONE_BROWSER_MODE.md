# Astro Phone Browser Mode

If you do not have an Expo account or Android Studio yet, you can still use Astro from your phone without installing an APK.

## How To Use

1. Open Astro Desktop.
2. Go to **Pair Phone**.
3. Click **Start Phone Pairing**.
4. On your phone, scan the QR code with your camera.
5. Open the link in your mobile browser.

## What Works

- Send typed commands to Astro Desktop.
- See Astro replies.
- See timers/reminders/setup notes.
- Use the protected Live PC Screen preview.
- Revoke phone access from the desktop app.

## Connection

Your phone and PC must be on the same Wi-Fi or hotspot network.

## Security

The link contains a pairing token. Do not share it. If you think someone else has it, use **Revoke All Phones** in Astro Desktop.
